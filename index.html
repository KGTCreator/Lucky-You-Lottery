What Is Lucky You Lottery?

We started back in the year 1892. when billionare leprecon, Patrick Leppy. had decided that it was unfair that so many around him didn't have the same good luck like he did. It took a lot of time, effort, and of course, luck, to get his new idea off the ground.

And so, he created a special lottery stystem to fix this. Naming it Lucky You Lottery, he designed it to offer all kinds of good luck to participants. The Top 5 are rewarded with the highest prizes. Even those that don't make it into the top three don't need to fear, as there are different small prizes for everyone that participacts in the lottery every week.

Patrick made sure, from that day forward, that his new lottery system could bring joy to many people. Even long after his death in 1925, his legacy lives on in his creation. We are dedicated to keeping his dream of giving people lucky alive, and now, here we are today!
